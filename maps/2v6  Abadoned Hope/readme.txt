2v6 Abandoned Hope

This island once was brimming with life ,but ever since the zero hour conflict it became a ghost town.
Your job here is simple: Retake the island back with your friend (or a bot) at your side 
against six other enemies who also want to take it for themselves.
Fortunately, there is a nearby hill with bunkers on top of it to rain down rockets on to your foes.

Good luck!
