A River Divided SURVIVAL  (AOD-style co-op wave defense)
Built from 'A River Divided (2x6) V5' (original untouched) for the
ShockWaveSPE + layers mod stack.

SETUP: 2 human players on the two NORTHERN start positions (slots 1-2).
No enemy AI players are needed -- the map spawns its own 30 escalating waves
from the six southern start zones (they hunt your bases across the river).
Adding AI players is allowed; waves are hostile to every lobby slot.

Difficulty = the game difficulty (as in AOD SWARM / Time To Kill):
easy/normal/hard changes starting cash ($30k/$20k/$10k) and hard adds
escort lanes on boss waves 10/20/30.

90 s build-up, then clear-gated waves (next wave 30 s after the previous is
wiped; a 300 s failsafe keeps the ladder moving if stragglers hide).
Wave-cleared bounty: $1000 + $200/wave to every player.
Boss waves every 10th. Victory after all 30 waves. Standard skirmish defeat.

Install: copy this folder into
~/Library/Application Support/GeneralsX/GeneralsZH/Maps/
