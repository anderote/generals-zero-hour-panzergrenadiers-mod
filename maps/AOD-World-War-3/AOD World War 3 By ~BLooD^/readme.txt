Max. Players: 4
Game: C&C Generals: Zero Hour
Type of Map: Art Of Defense (AOD)

Description:
This map is designed for 1-4 players to play against the GLA AI. 
The AI must be placed at the top right corner of the map.

You can choose the difficulty of the map, which will affect the starting money,
and the special powers that the AI can use against you during the game.

Be cautious of attacks on your base and pay attention to the roads, as they are paths for the AI.

Discover the rest of the map and its features during the game. Good luck!

VICTORY:
Players must defeat the final boss.

DEFEAT;
Players will be defeated if the AI enters the area called "END" at the end of the map.

If you encounter any bugs or mistakes on the map, please feel free to contact me.
I welcome your opinions and feedback.
